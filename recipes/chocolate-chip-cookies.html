Joy of Cooking Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe



Description

This is one of the best chocolate chip cookie recipes I've ever found! Joy of Cooking has created the best richest, tastiest cookie to rule them all!

Prep time: 10 min, Bake time: 8 - 10 min

Ingredients

  • Half cup unsalted butter, room temp
  • Half cup granulated sugar
  • Half cup brown sugar
  • One egg
  • One cup and two tbsp flour
  • Half tsp baking sodas
  • Fourth tsp alt
  • One and a half tsp vanilla
  • One cup chocolate chips
  • Optional: three-fourths cup walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Prepare baking pan
  2. Mix together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar
  3. Mix in egg
  4. Mix in flour, baking soda, salt, and vanilla
  5. Mix in chocolate chips
  6. Scoop onto the prepared pan (about tbsp in size), leave about 2 inches between each cookie
  7. Bake 8-12 minutes or until cookies are golden brown
  8. Transfer to rack to cool
  9. Enjoy
